# Pharma and Counterfeit Drug Prevention Supply Chain and Smart Contracts System in Blockchain

## 📌 Project Overview

A Blockchain-based Pharmaceutical Supply Chain Management System designed to prevent counterfeit drugs and ensure transparency throughout the medicine distribution process.

The system enables secure tracking of medicines from manufacturers to distributors, wholesalers, pharmacies, and end consumers using blockchain technology and smart contracts.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Prevent counterfeit drug circulation
* Improve transparency in the pharmaceutical supply chain
* Ensure secure medicine tracking
* Maintain immutable records using blockchain
* Enhance trust among stakeholders
